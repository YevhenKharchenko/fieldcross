About the Game

Idle Market Tycoon — Mini Mart lets you build up your store with upgrades that pretty much handle themselves. By stocking shelves, unlocking departments, hiring staff, and reinvesting profits, players transform a small shop into a thriving retail business. Even offline, earnings continue to accumulate, play smart and you’ll be rolling in dough.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Start with a basic mini mart layout

  • Unlock new product aisles

  • Hire and upgrade employees

  • Increase checkout efficiency

  • Earn idle profits and reinvest

The whole point is making your shop bigger and getting the most out of every sale.

Core Features

  • Money keeps rolling in even when you're not playing

  • Store expansion upgrades

  • Simple tap-based controls

  • Reward-based progression system

  • You'll actually see your store change as you play

  • Staff management mechanics
